welcome fellow otaku

Who are we?

We are a group of various people who love ANIME. We are purely here to spread good words and our love for ANIME, and to share to you our beloved characters. We are open to whoever would like to join us in this growing community to spread love and good vibes over a hobby that is sometimes look down upon.

"Over the years, some people do not see the value of watching an anime. But we are here to make them see the full glory of being an Otaku, may you be over or under 18 years old."
-DDLP

So why do we do it?

First and foremost, watching an anime is fun. At first glance, looking at a moving character is purely amazing. It is very hard to draw a moving character and it is less praised by many people. Second, you can sometimes relate to the characters life stories. Like some are bullied, the bullies has some sad stories, and also the love story that makes us want to weep our hearts out.

Third, you sometimes want to escape reality and this is the outlet for it. By just imagining that you are one of the MC (main character) or the SC (supporting character). Don't deny it. We have fantasies of our own. No one never has a fantasy before. We want to escape life even just for a little while. Fourth, we also want them to become a reality. Having those superpowers are so cool that you want to either be friends with them or you want to learn how to do those amazing powers. we want to rule this world with just a simple flick of our fingers.

Lastly, and the most important of them all, is to give the voice actors and the creators of ANIME (manga and the animations) the praise they higly deserve. The voice actor who we never get to see gives life to the characters that we love. They act through their voice which is amazing enough that they can convey what the authors want the charcters feels. And the creators and animators for imaging such story line and drawing the characters to fit the stories. They work so hard to make their work known. To catch a single interested reader is hard, let alone hundreds, thousands, even millions of readers and watchers. We OTAKUS just don't watch ANIME for fun, but to praise the GODS of the creation they shined down upon us.
